Query flood
Client sending >100 queries in 10 seconds — REFUSED. After 3 consecutive violations → blocked for 5 minutes.

Client generating >50 NXDOMAIN responses in 10 seconds — blocked immediately for 5 minutes. Indicates subdomain scanning or malware.
Block response
Blocked clients receive REFUSED (rcode 5) for all queries. Blocks auto-expire and persist across server restarts.
